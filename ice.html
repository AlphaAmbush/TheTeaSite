Summer time

Lemongrass ginger iced tea

Prep time: 3 min.
Cook: 35 min.
Total: 38 min.
Ingredients: 5.

Iced tea is the perfect drink for summer and it can quickly be dressed up to create an even better and more impressive drink. Many of the best iced tea recipes are also the easiest, so don't think that you have to stick with that same tea you've been drinking for years.

Ingredients


  1. 2 cups filtered water
  2. large stalk lemongrass
  3. 1 to 2 teaspoons sugar
  4. 1 cup ice, optional
  5. inch x 2 inch knob ginger, peeled and chopped
  6. mild black tea bags

Steps to follow

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Bring 2 cups water to a boil.
  3. Combine the sugar, 1/2 cup water, lemongrass and ginger.
  4. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 2 minutes. Remove from the heat and allow the lemongrass and ginger to steep in the sugar syrup for at least half an hour.
  5. Bring 2 cups of water to a boil. Add the teabags, turn off the heat, and allow the tea to steep for 5 minutes.
  6. Strain the lemongrass ginger syrup through a small sieve, pressing on the solids to extract as much flavor as possible.
  7. Add three cups of cold water to the tea, and stir well. Chill the tea, and serve over ice. Enjoy!
