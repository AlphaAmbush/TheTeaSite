Sit along tea

It's your opportunity to create a moment: for stillness, for reflection, for yourself. It's your connection to a world of senses; flavors both exotic and familiar, energizing and relaxing. It's your retreat from an increasingly turbulent world. It is the perfect paradox of simplicity and complexity

Tea is an aromatic beverage prepared by pouring hot or boiling water over cured or fresh leaves of Camellia sinensis, an evergreen shrub native to China and other East Asian countries.
After water, it is the most widely consumed drink in the world.
There are many different types of tea; some, like Chinese greens and Darjeeling, have a cooling, slightly bitter, and astringent flavour, while others have vastly different profiles that include sweet, nutty, floral, or grassy notes.
Tea has a stimulating effect in humans primarily due to its caffeine content.
