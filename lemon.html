Lemon ginger green tea

Prep time: 3 min.
Cook: 4 min.
Total: 7 min.
Ingredients: 4.

This green tea recipe has the health benefits and zingy flavor of ginger plus the refreshing citrus taste of lemon thanks to lemon-ginger simple syrup.

Ingredients


  1. 1 tablespoon green tea
  2. 1 cup simmering water
  3. 2 teaspoons Lemon-Ginger Simple Syrup
  4. (For iced tea) 1 cup ice

Steps to follow

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Steep the tea for three minutes.
  3. Strain and add Lemon-Ginger Simple Syrup.
  4. Serve hot or pour over ice.
