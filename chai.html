Masala chai Tea

Prep time: 5 min.
Cook: 15 min.
Total: 20 min.
Ingredients: 9.

Masala chai is a comforting and warming drink with one-third the caffeine as coffee. It is perfect for an after-dinner or before-bed beverage.

Ingredients


  1. 2 cups milk, or milk substitute
  2. 2 cups water
  3. 4 whole cloves
  4. 2 pods green cardamom, crushed
  5. 2 peppercorns, crushed
  6. 1 cinnamon stick
  7. 1 1/2-inch piece ginger, peeled and chopped or grated
  8. 2 tablespoons sugar
  9. 2 tablespoons â€‹black tea leaves

Steps to follow

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. In a medium saucepan, combine the milk, water, and spices. Simmer over medium heat for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add the sugar and tea leaves. Stir, and then simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Strain into glasses or mugs and serve.
